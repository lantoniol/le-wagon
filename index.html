rat removal

With more than 6 million individuals estimated in the capital, rats are becoming a real phenomenon in Paris. According to statistics, today there are about 1.75 rats per inhabitant. Rodents are reproducing at an incredible speed, jeopardizing the hygiene of the capital. Paris is in general the image of large cities. They offer conditions conducive to the proliferation of rats and mice: presence of food, water, space to nest.
